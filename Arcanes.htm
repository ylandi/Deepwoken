Prosthetic skills[]

Skills that can be obtained by talking to Elaria, located in whispering pines while having a prosthetic equipped.
Flamethrower Shoot out fire from your prosthetic, applying burn
Shuriken-throw Leap above, throwing a barrage of explosive shurikens towards your cursor. Applies burn.
Obliterate Charge your prosthesis, overloading it and causing a massive explosion. Applies a special type of burn, has a chance to break legs and sever arms.Requires you to have Ein Greus equipped and 100 soulfrags

NPC skills[]

Skills obtained through mob drops from hostile NPCs.
One mind Dash forward, slicing them then sheathing your blade to unleash a flurry of slashes upon your foe.Obtainment: Low drop chance from the shattered enemy npcs, Spawns in the Shattered event and in the Battletower event.
Palm strikes N/A Grab your enemy and then knock them away dealing decent damage with a short cooldown.Obtainment: Low drop chance from Faelan, who spawns after 30 minutes of creating a server. He spawns below the second tree to the left when facing the Payload villager.

Affinity skills[]

Obtained from Melior, located in Whispering Pines with a requirement stat of 25-30 affinity and 1000 nebulae.

(50 affinity, Mana Core, level 25, 1500 nebulae for Max output)

Skills obtained by npcs with the affinity stat.
Mana blast After a small windup, fire a powerful beam of mana from your hands.
Maximum: mana output After condensing your mana in your hands, fire it as a devastating blast.

Psionics skills[]

Skills obtained through NPCs with the psionics stat.

Obtained through Faelith, located near the castle at the cost of 1000 nebulae and a requirement of 30 psionics.

Skills obtained through NPCs with the psionics stat.
Fall manifest your psionic prowess into the world, showing your mastery over it and forcing everyone hit to kneel before you.

Agility skills[]

Skills obtained through NPCs with the agility stat.

Acquired through Qix with a requirement of 15 agility.

Skills obtained through NPCs with the agility stat.
Kick-step vanish with your immense speed, before delivering a swift kick.
